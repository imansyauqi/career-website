Who We Are

ZAINOL NABAWI ENTERPRISE (formerly known as SUMBERSARI ENTERPRISE) has more than 17 years in the service of supplying contract workers. The company focuses only supplying services contract workers for all sectors of industry.

OUR MISSION

OUR VISION

Ready supply of labour be efficient, quality and timely.

Our Values

Commitment to Excellence

We strive to provide high-quality, efficient, and timely labor solutions. With over 17 years of experience, we ensure that our workforce meets industry standards while exceeding client expectations.

Client-Focused Solutions

Our mission is to assist and facilitate our clients in managing and planning their workforce. We prioritize understanding the unique needs of each customer, ensuring tailored and reliable labor solutions for every sector.

Dependability and Integrity

We are dedicated to being a trusted partner, offering a ready supply of workers with a focus on professionalism, transparency, and reliability. Our long-standing presence in the industry reflects our commitment to building lasting relationships based on trust.
